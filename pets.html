FREEFIRE PET'S

Mr.Waggor

Ability Smooth Gloo: Produces Gloo Wall grenades in Clash Squad, and provides Gloo Wall EXP in Battle Royale.Mr Waggor can generate 1 Gloo Wall grenade every (120/100/100) seconds. The penguin pet also comes with two outfits and three lovely emotes for players to unlock at different levels.

ROBO

Robo is the most powerful pet in Free Fire with an ability called Wall Enforcement. This pet adds shields to the gloo walls and helps recover 60 HP. Since there are two characters, Skyler and Xayne, who can destroy gloo walls, Robo becomes exceptionally useful when it comes to adding an extra layer of protection.

PANDA

Detective Panda is one of the most popular pets in Free Fire that many players use on the battlefield. The pet costs 699 diamonds and allows users to get an HP recovery after each kill. At the first level, players receive a restoration of four HP upon each kill.

FALCO

Falco is a pet in Free Fire, a popular battle royale game. Falco is the first flying pet in the game, and it has a special ability called Skyline Spree. This ability increases the gliding speed of the player and their teammates when they skydive and dive after the parachute opens.

OTTERO

Ottero is a pet in Garena Free Fire, a popular battle royale game. Ottero is a cute beaver with a hip-hop style, wearing a headphone and a hoodie. Ottero has a special ability called Double Bubble, which allows it to increase your EP (energy points) whenever you use a Treatment Gun or a Med Kit to heal your HP (health points).

BEASTON

Beaston is a new pet in Free Fire that was introduced with the OB25 update and the Operation Chrono event. It is a baboon-inspired pet that has a unique skill called Helping Hand. This skill allows you to throw grenades, gloo walls, flashbangs, and smoke grenades farther than usual. The distance increases as you level up the pet.

ROCKIE

Rockie’s skill is called Stay Chill, which decreases the cooldown time of the equipped active skill by 6% at the first level. This skill is further enhanced with the increase in the pet’s level. At the highest level, the cooldown time is reduced by 15%.

NIGHT PANTHER

Night Panther’s skill is called Weight Training, which can increase your inventory space by 15 at level 1, and up to 45 at level 7. Night Panther is a good companion for support players, as they can carry more items and supplies for their team.

SPIRIT FOX

Spirit Fox’s ability can give the player an advantage by providing extra HPs while applying medkits, allowing them to recover faster and ambush enemies quicker. The amount of extra HP and the cooldown time depend on the level of Spirit Fox.
