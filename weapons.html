WEAPONS

RGS50

RGS50 is a Launcher with 40mm Ammo. The weapon has a high area of effect damage and has a very long range. It also have a very high accuarcy but it has a slow rate of fire.

M249

The M249 Light Machine Gun (formally 5.56 mm, M249; also known as the M249 Squad Automatic Weapon) is the American version of the Belgian FN Minimi light machine gun, manufactured by FNH USA and used by all branches of the United States Armed Forces.

GATLING

Gatling gun, hand-driven machine gun, the first to solve the problems of loading, reliability, and the firing of sustained bursts. It was invented about 1862 by Richard Jordan Gatling during the American Civil War .

MP40

The MP 40 ( Maschinenpistole 40) is a submachine gun chambered for the 9×19mm Parabellum cartridge. It was developed in Nazi Germany and used extensively by the Axis powers during World War II .

UMP

The Heckler & Koch UMP is a submachine gun developed and manufactured by Heckler & Koch. Heckler & Koch developed the UMP as a lighter and cheaper successor to the MP5, though both remain in production.

M1887

M1887 is a Shotgun with SG Ammo. The weapon has a very high damage and a very slow rate of fire. The weapon has the highest range between shotguns with a short range, but the weapon has a very fast reload time.

KATANA

Katana is a Melee Weapon with no Ammo. Katana has a damage of 66 and a high accuracy. The weapon can be used as a shield to protect the wearer, until it eventually gets destroyed by the bullets.

GENADE

A grenade is an explosive weapon typically thrown by hand (also called hand grenade), but can also refer to a shell (explosive projectile) shot from the muzzle of a rifle (as a rifle grenade) or a grenade launcher.

KAR98K

The Karabiner 98 kurz often abbreviated Karabiner 98k, Kar98k or K98k and also sometimes incorrectly referred to as a K98 . is a bolt-action rifle chambered for the 7.92×57mm Mauser cartridge.
